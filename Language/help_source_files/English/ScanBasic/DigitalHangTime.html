Digital Hang Time

Sets the time the radio will remain on a digital scan list member following the end of the channel activity. The hang time prevents the radio from resuming scanning until the conclusion of the response to the initial call. The timer starts at the end of a transmission and resets whenever a valid activity is detected on the digital channel during the hang time.

Range
Max10000
Min500
Step500

Note
  • If the call interruption timer of the terminal intercom is increased, it is recommended to increase the hold time value.
