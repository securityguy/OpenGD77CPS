Squelch

Filters incoming signals that are not strong enough to produce a clear transmission, thereby eliminating unwanted noise. This feature adjusts the squelch threshold of an incoming transmission. This feature can be toggled between tight or normal squelch, via a short or long programmable button press (Tight/Normal Squelch) or Squelch (Utilities Menu). This is a channel-wide feature.

Option
NormalUnmutes to incoming transmission with a normal signal strength.
TightUnmutes to incoming transmission with a tight signal strength.
